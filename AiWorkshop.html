About the India–AI Impact Summit 2026

India will host the AI Impact Summit in New Delhi on 19–20 February 2026, bringing together global leaders, researchers, innovators, and policymakers to shape a future-oriented agenda for inclusive, responsible, and impactful AI cooperation.

The Summit aims to ensure that Artificial Intelligence becomes a driver of:

  • Inclusive human development
  • Environmental sustainability
  • Equitable global progress

By bridging the Global AI Divide, the Summit seeks to promote AI solutions that strengthen communities, protect the planet, and accelerate progress toward a more just and sustainable future for all.

About the Pre-Summit Workshop at CMPICA

This workshop aims to explore the transformative role of AI in enhancing diagnostics, early disease detection, and supporting preventive care. It will bring together experts from healthcare, academia, and technology domains to share insights on:

  • AI-driven precision medicine
  • Medical imaging & predictive analytics
  • AI-assisted clinical decision-making
  • Ethical & responsible healthcare innovation
