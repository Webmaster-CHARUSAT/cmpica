Welcome To CMPICA

Smt. Chandaben Mohanbhai Patel Institute of Computer Applications is offering a three-year full-time Master of Computer Applications (MCA) Program with the intake of 60 students per year, a two-year full-time MCA Lateral Program with an intake of 120 students per year, a two-year full-time Master of Science in Information Technology (M.Sc.(IT)) Program with intake of 60 students per year, a five-year full-time Dual Degree Master of Computer Applications (MCA-Dual Degree BCA + MCA) Program with intake of 60 students per year and a Doctor of Philosophy (Ph. D.) Program (Full time/Part time).
